About Me

My Story

I’m Alessio, a front-end developer with a background that’s anything but conventional. After nearly a decade working in hospitality in London — leading high-performing teams and managing demanding environments — I decided to reinvent myself and pursue a long-time passion for technology. In 2023, I started studying web development while working full-time, gradually transitioning into a more creative and fulfilling career path. This journey taught me resilience, discipline, and the importance of building things that are both functional and meaningful. Now, I help individuals and businesses bring their ideas online through intuitive, responsive, and visually engaging websites.

My Tech Stack

I build modern, responsive websites and web applications with a strong focus on clean code, accessibility, and user experience.

Languages & Tools:
  • HTML5 / CSS3 / JavaScript (ES6+)
  • React (with hooks)
  • Django & Django REST Framework
  • PostgreSQL
  • Bootstrap / AOS / Custom CSS
  • Git / GitHub
  • Deployment via Heroku, Netlify, and Namecheap (custom domains)

What I Build

I mostly work with small businesses, professionals, and digital creatives to create:

  • Portfolio websites for individuals looking to showcase their work
  • Restaurant websites with table booking systems
  • Landing pages for new products or local businesses
  • Educational games and interactive experiences (like Pictures and Places)
  • Custom dashboards or admin panels with Django backends
